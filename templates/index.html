Welcome to Townhall.digital!

Townhall.digital is written in Python3 and hosted with Flask framework. The project is designed for users who want to automatically create facebook group with a graphical user-interface. Project developed by Edmond Tsoi and Dominick Huyn, supervised by Professor Elliot Soloway and Professor Idit Adler.
